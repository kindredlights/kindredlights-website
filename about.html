A Life, Not a Degree

You can't get here from there.

Mapmaker

I didn't develop the Five StepsRecognize. Relax. Release. Receive. Respond. and the Eight AspectsCalm. Peace. Power. Love. Joy. Wisdom. Light. Expression. in a retreat center or a graduate program. I built them in custody courtrooms, on jail floors, beside my teenager in crisis, and in ten thousand ordinary moments where my lower self wanted to burn the world down — and my higher self said: open your heart. I'll show you what to do.

The Beginning

The jail floor was mine.

Three DUIs. A narcissistic marriage. A custody battle that cost me daily access to my children for years. Public shaming at a scale that would have been career-ending in any conventional sense. I was not a tragic figure in these moments — I was a soul in the exact crucible her KarmariculumYour soul's custom lesson plan. had designed. I just didn't know that yet.

I had to find the Wise OneYour higher self. Already home. immediately. There was simply no other way through. And what I found there changed everything — not just for me, but for everyone around me.

What I discovered in that rebuilding — slowly, stepping stone by stepping stone — was not a coping mechanism or a self-improvement strategy. It was a completely different way of being. One that didn't ask Sir EgoYour ego. The hero with the wrong map. to be better. It asked him to step aside so something wiser could come through.

The Proof

From many messes to many miracles.

Twenty years of sobriety. Three children who are thriving. A nonprofit career that turned professional catastrophe into a platform for genuine service. And the miracle I could not have imagined on that jail floor: a Thanksgiving table rebuilt from wreckage — with a seat at the head of it for the man who had once been the adversary in a brutal custody battle.

That table is not a metaphor. It is a real Thanksgiving. A real family. A real forgiveness that required real work — five steps, one trigger at a time, for years. Colin Tipping — the creator of Radical Forgiveness — called it one of the most powerful demonstrations of his work he had ever witnessed. The forgiveness circle in these pages is his. The table that resulted from it is ours.

"Not perfectly. But heroically." This is the only credential that matters. The proof is a life.

The Teaching

I made the map. You walk it.

I'm not a guru. I'm not a master. I'm a mapmaker.

I went through bad stuff — addiction, a marriage that dismantled my sense of reality, losing my children for years, a jail floor, the slow work of rebuilding from nothing. And in the middle of all of that, I had to find something that would actually work. Not later. Not in the calm between storms. In the fire. In the triggered moment. While the freight train was already moving.

What I found became the Five Steps. What I kept finding as I walked them became Luminology — Sir Ego, the Wise One, the Karmariculum, the Eight Aspects. It isn't academic. It isn't borrowed. It's the map I drew as I walked. I'm handing it to you now.

Why Now

We are on the collective jail floor.

As I've watched the world expose the egoic patterns at the root of all our problems — the polarization, the narcissism in leadership, the collective suffering — I felt the familiar sting. I've seen this before. In my own life, at volume 10. And I know what comes next when the Wise One is finally allowed through.

Humanity is headed for a Thanksgiving reunion that blesses everyone. I've seen what transformation from ego consciousness to Wise One consciousness produces — miracles in our inner world, our outer worlds, and our relationships. The age that is ending makes the age that is arising possible.

That is why this work exists now. Not because the timing is convenient. Because the world is ready to hear it — and because someone who has already walked this path needs to hold the light up for everyone still in the dark.
