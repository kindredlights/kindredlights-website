Beyond Blame & Shame

The Truth of Who You Are

What if blame was never the truth — and shame was never the truth — and both arose from the same foundational misunderstanding about who you are and why you came here?

Beyond Blame & Shame is a complete introduction to Luminology — the Eight AspectsCalm. Peace. Power. Love. Joy. Wisdom. Light. Expression. of the Divine and the journey home. Nine chapters, one for each of the eight exalted aspects of your eternal nature, plus the complete cosmological foundation. Each one follows a soul through the crucible of its forging.

This is not a book about concepts. It is a book about what it actually looks like when a divine being in a human body finds its way from Sir EgoYour ego. The hero with the wrong map.'s distortions back to the Wise OneYour higher self. Already home.'s truth. From the holding cell to the open heart. From blame and shame to the truth of who you are.

Nine Chapters
Ch. 1 Luminology — The complete cosmology
Ch. 2 Light — From hiding to radiant presence
Ch. 3 Peace — From anxious control to unshakeable faith
Ch. 4 Calm — From reactivity to the still point
Ch. 5 Wisdom — From defended opinions to clear discernment

Ch. 7 Power — From force to sovereign co-creation
Ch. 8 Joy — From bliss-lack to causeless delight

Coming · The Memoir

The Mastery of Sir Ego

& the Rise of the Wise One — A Memoir

Three DUIs. A jail floor. A custody battle with a narcissist. Four hours a week with three children. And then: twenty years of sobriety. Three children thriving. A Thanksgiving table rebuilt from wreckage — including a seat at the head of it for the man who had once been the adversary.

The Mastery of Sir Ego is the proof of concept in a single life. Not inspirational distance — the actual mud, documented honestly. The Five Steps and the Eight Aspects were not developed in a retreat center or a graduate program. They were built in custody courtrooms, in supervised visitation rooms, beside a teenager in crisis, and in ten thousand ordinary moments where the lower self wanted to burn the world down and the higher self said: open your heart. I'll show you what to do.

This is the only spiritual memoir that hands you something usable on the way out. Every domain. Every step. The proof is a life.
