The Map Minder

Sometimes things go off course.

We each have a subconscious script of how everything should be. But situations and people are always going off script — because they all have their own. The Map Minder reveals the next steps on the path that aligns with your life’s divine plan and is the highest good for everyone.

Your higher self guides the way ✦

These two dream metaphors will show you how the Map Minder works.

The Two Caves

I had a dream that I was bouncing back and forth between two caves: the first is dark and scary and the other is OK — not great, but OK. I didn’t understand why I kept bouncing back and forth, so I tried to crack the code so that I could stay in the OK cave. As children, this is how we start to build a script to keep us in the OK cave. It seems to work. Until it doesn’t.

The script sounds like: “If I just please people, they will love me.” Or: “This is how good people act.” Or: “This is how I get what I want.”

This was our programming and social conditioning, and it taught us to mold ourselves into acceptable versions of our true self. Every script is now the source of the problem.

But there is a place beyond the two caves — a better life. Our life’s divine plan offers us opportunities to cast off this script, reveal our true selves and live our highest path. Our inner guide knows the way. When we follow her.

I was walking through paradise with the Creator. The monkeys were shrieking — loud, chaotic, impossible to ignore. I wished it would stop. And it did. Then the consequences fell like dominoes. No monkeys. No mating. No babies. Next, I wished the nearby lion wouldn’t eat the gazelle. Lions starved. Gazelles overpopulated and ate everything green. Paradise went barren.

All because I cast my opinions and fears upon the divine perfection that I did not see or understand.

Our lower self judges according to our script where lions are vegetarian. Our higher self sees the next lesson in our life’s divine plan. The situation you want to fix might be doing exactly what it is supposed to do — in ways you cannot yet see.

What if the world is already perfect — and your inner state is where the work is?

This works best if you are open to the possibility that what is happening might be part of a larger plan — even when it doesn’t feel that way. Willingness and receptivity are the only prerequisites.

Now — tell the Wise One what's not moving.

Two or three sentences. A situation. A relationship. A pattern. That thing that keeps showing up in different costumes.

What do you think SHOULD be happening instead?

This is Sir Ego's script. Name it honestly. She should appreciate me more. He should listen. This shouldn't have happened to me.
