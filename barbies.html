Coming Soon · Sacred & Irreverent

Barbie
lives in the Land
of Should.

Until it turns into the Land of Should Not.

A short-form video series where the Five Steps get real on a Tuesday afternoon. Twenty-seven Barbies, each running her own ego-script — Hustle Barbie, Doomscroll Barbie, Pleaser Barbie, Outrage Barbie, Conspiracy Barbie, Empath Barbie, Sober Barbie, Triggered Barbie, and twenty more — all walking the same path home. One trigger at a time. One Wise One choosing differently. One Tuesday at a time.

The Barbies are how the Five Steps reach the people the books can't.

Not everyone is going to read a 300-page book. Most people are not. Most people are scrolling at 9pm on a Tuesday after a hard day, looking for something that lands in 90 seconds. The Barbies meet them there.

Each episode is a short video — 7 slides, about a minute and a half — featuring a different Barbie, each one running a different version of the same script the books are mapping at length. Hustle Barbie cannot stop. Outrage Barbie is on fire. Pleaser Barbie has lost herself in caretaking. Conspiracy Barbie has all the answers. Empath Barbie is drowning in everyone else's feelings. Sober Barbie is on day 3 and white-knuckling it. Each Barbie meets the trigger that fires her particular script and walks the Five Steps in real time, in language that fits her terrain. Then the next Barbie, on a different day, walks the same steps through her own.

The same teaching. Different costumes. The reader who recognizes herself in Hustle Barbie has just received the framework that lives at full depth in Love Heroically and What Happened to US? — without having read either one. The seed is planted. Sometimes that is enough. Sometimes it is the beginning.

Sacred and irreverent, both. The work is ministry. The voice is a friend on the couch. Twenty-seven Barbies because there is room in this for all of us — the ones who are righteous, the ones who are anxious, the ones who are exhausted, the ones who are angry, the ones who have given up. Barbie has been all of us at one point or another. The Five Steps walk her, and us, home.
