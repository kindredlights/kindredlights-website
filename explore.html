The Exploratorium

Explore

You try hard. You care deeply. You've been thoughtful about this for a long time — more than anyone around you probably realizes.

And still there are things. The old ones that won't quite resolve no matter what you do. The new ones that show up out of nowhere, sometimes at the worst possible moment. Some days you handle them. Some days you don't. Afterward, the part of you that knew better was right there the whole time — and you can't figure out why you couldn't reach her.

These rooms are a place to explore what's actually going on. Wander where you like. Stay with what catches you. Come back when something else calls. The Wise OneYour higher self. Already home. is here too — she's always here — and these pages are laid out to make her easier to find.
